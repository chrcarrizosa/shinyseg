This panel shows the existing pedigrees. You can shift through them by using the / buttons.

In the graph, each individual is colored according to its phenotype (? if unknown) and the symbols below denote the carriership status (−: non-carrier; +: heterozygous or hemizygous carrier; ++: homozygous carrier; empty: unknown).

The numbers indicate ages of onset or censoring, and the arrow ↗ denotes the proband(s) of the analysis.
